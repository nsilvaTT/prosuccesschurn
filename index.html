Key Takeaways
📉
At-risk rate dropped sharply — validate with QA
Combined at-risk fell from 23.1% → 16.0% (−7.1 pp). Zero calls had explicit churn intent this cycle — all 43 at-risk calls are "Unclear." Positive signal, but worth validating via manual call review before drawing firm conclusions on pro health.
📈
Retention attempts trending up for second cycle
Attempts rose from 14.8% → 17.9% (+3.1 pp) — second consecutive improvement. When reps try, they succeed roughly 1 in 4 times. Success rate held steady at ~25%. Priority: help reps recognize the "Unclear" signal earlier.
⚠
Lead ROI is now the #1 churn driver
Among at-risk calls, Low ROI on Leads jumped to #1 at 37.2% (up from ~15% last cycle). Pricing/Cost dropped from ~30% to 14%. Coach reps to address "I'm spending money on leads but not getting jobs" objections specifically.
🎯
Retention success CI is wide — small sample
25% success (12/48) has a ±12.3 pp confidence interval. The true rate could be anywhere from 12.7% to 37.3%. Treat as directional only — need 3–4 more cycles to establish a reliable baseline.
